About Our Salon

Discover the story behind our passion for beauty and excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by master stylist Sarah Johnson, Glamour Salon began as a small boutique studio with a big vision: to create a space where clients could receive top-quality hair services in a relaxing, luxurious environment.

What started as a one-chair operation has grown into one of the city's most sought-after salons, with a team of 10 talented professionals specializing in all aspects of hair care and styling.
