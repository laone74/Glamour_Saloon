Frequently Asked Questions

You can book an appointment by calling us at (212) 555-1234, using our online booking system, or filling out the contact form on this page. We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for popular time slots.

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the appointment may be subject to a fee of 50% of the service cost. No-shows will be charged the full amount of the scheduled service.

We do accept walk-ins based on availability, but appointments are strongly recommended to ensure you get the time slot and stylist you prefer. Walk-ins are typically seen within 30 minutes if we have availability.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), cash, and mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay). We also accept gift certificates purchased through our salon.

Yes! Gift certificates are available in any denomination and can be purchased in-salon or over the phone. They never expire and can be used for any service or product we offer. Perfect for birthdays, holidays, or just because!
